1. Service Terms

1.1 Scope of Work

Sagar Borewells agrees to provide drilling machinery, labor, and standard equipment. The quote provided online is an estimate only. Final billing will be based on the actual depth drilled in feet and the quantity of casing pipe used.

1.2 Payment Structure

  • Mobilization Advance: ₹5,000 to ₹10,000 must be paid to confirm the booking date.
  • Drilling Payment: 80% of the estimated cost must be paid when the rig arrives at the site.
  • Final Settlement: The remaining balance must be cleared immediately upon completion of drilling, before the rig leaves the site.

1.3 No Water Guarantee

Drilling is a geological exploration. While we use advanced sensors to predict water zones, we do not guarantee the availability, yield, or quality of water. The customer is liable to pay full drilling charges even if the point is dry (Dust Point).

2. Additional Operating Guidelines

2.1 Site Clearance (Client Responsibility)

The client must ensure a clear path (min 12ft width) for the rig truck to enter. Any damage to gates, walls, or trees during entry/exit is the client's risk. Overhead electrical wires must be cleared.

2.2 Government Permits

The client is solely responsible for obtaining necessary permissions (WALTA Act, Municipal approvals, Ground Water Dept) prior to drilling. Sagar Borewells is not liable for any legal stoppages or fines.

2.3 Material Safety

Once casing pipes and pumps are delivered to the site, their safety from theft or damage is the client's responsibility.

2.4 Waste Disposal

Drilling mud and dust will be deposited near the bore point. Cleaning, leveling, or removing this mud is not included in the standard package unless "Site Cleaning" service is booked separately.
