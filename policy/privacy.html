Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

When you use our Estimate Engine, we collect your Name, Phone Number, and Location (GPS Coordinates). This is strictly to provide you with a Quote.

2. Usage of Data

We use your phone number to:

  • Send you the PDF Estimate via WhatsApp.
  • Call you for the expert site verification.
  • We NEVER sell your data to third parties.

3. Location Services

Our "Detect GPS" feature uses your browser's geolocation API. This data is processed instantly to determine your Zone (Soil Type) and is not stored permanently on our servers after the session ends.
